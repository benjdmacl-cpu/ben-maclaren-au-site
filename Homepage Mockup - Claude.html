Approach

Presence, before proof.

I came to counselling after years in product design, journalism, and group facilitation — work that taught me how to sit with people while they figure out what's actually going on, rather than rushing them toward an answer.

I'm not here to fix the presenting problem. I'm here to understand the whole person it belongs to.

Sessions are non-directive and exploratory. You set the pace, I stay close to what's actually being said, and we follow what matters rather than a script.
