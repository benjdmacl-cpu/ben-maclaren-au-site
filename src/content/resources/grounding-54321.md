title: '5-4-3-2-1 grounding'
segment: 'mindfulness'
intro: "A quick sensory grounding exercise for when things are moving too fast and you need to land back in the room you're actually in."
interactionType: ['worksheet']
icon: 'hand-finger'
instructionsLabel: 'How it works'
steps:
  - title: 'Name five things you can see'
    body: 'Just look around and name them, silently or out loud.'
  - title: 'Name four things you can touch'
    body: 'Notice the texture of each one as you name it.'
  - title: 'Name three things you can hear'
    body: 'Include sounds you might normally tune out.'
  - title: 'Name two things you can smell'
    body: "If nothing's obvious, notice the absence of smell."
  - title: 'Name one thing you can taste'
    body: 'Even just the inside of your own mouth counts.'
