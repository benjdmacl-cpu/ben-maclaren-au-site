segment: 'act-therapy'
intro: 'A printable sorting exercise for working out which values actually matter to you, separate from the ones you feel you''re supposed to have.'
interactionType: ['worksheet']
icon: 'compass'
instructionsLabel: 'How it works'
steps:
  - title: 'Print and cut out the cards'
    body: 'Each card names one value. Cut them into a stack you can physically sort.'
  - title: 'Sort into three piles'
    body: 'Very important, somewhat important, not important right now.'
  - title: 'Narrow the top pile to five'
    body: "It should feel hard. If it doesn't, you haven't narrowed enough."
  - title: 'Write one sentence per value'
    body: 'What would actually change this week if you took this value seriously.'
